Overview

The CarCare+ Application is designed to assist vehicle owners in efficiently managing and tracking the health and maintenance needs of their vehicles. The platform offers features such as mileage tracking, tire pressure monitoring, and timely reminders for essential vehicle services like maintenance, insurance, and taxes. It aims to simplify the upkeep of vehicles by integrating notifications, scheduling options for inspections, and detailed reporting on vehicle performance and costs. By leveraging AI, the application personalizes recommendations, predicts maintenance needs, and optimizes vehicle performance, ultimately providing a user-friendly solution for vehicle management.

Objectives

The objective of the Vehicle Health and Maintenance Management Application is to provide vehicle owners with a comprehensive and user-friendly platform to monitor their vehicle's health, manage routine maintenance, and optimize overall performance. The application aims to enhance vehicle longevity by offering timely reminders, predictive maintenance insights, and personalized recommendations through the use of artificial intelligence. By simplifying maintenance tracking, payment schedules, and reporting, the project seeks to improve the efficiency and convenience of vehicle ownership while minimizing costs and potential breakdowns.

Features

  • Mileage & Tire Pressure Tracking: Allows manual or sensor-based input for vehicle mileage and tire pressure, with reminders for maintenance and alerts for low pressure.
  • Maintenance & Payment Reminders: Provides personalized notifications for maintenance schedules, taxes, insurance, and payments, using AI to optimize reminders.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Enables users to book inspection and service appointments, with AI suggesting the best times and locations.
  • Store Integration & Recommendations: Offers a marketplace for vehicle-related products, with personalized recommendations based on user habits and vehicle needs.
  • Reporting & Predictive Maintenance: Generates detailed reports on vehicle performance, costs, and fuel consumption, with AI-powered predictive maintenance suggestions.

Technologies

  • Mileage & Sensor Integration: Implement manual input and sensor integration for mileage and tire pressure using real-time data processing techniques.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Leverage machine learning algorithms for predictive maintenance, personalized reminders, and vehicle performance optimization.
  • Notification System: Use scheduling algorithms and AI to send timely reminders for maintenance, payments, and inspections based on user behavior.
  • Web Development: Develop the application using .NET Core for backend services, with Angular for a responsive and dynamic frontend interface.
  • Data Analytics: Implement AI-powered analytics to generate reports on vehicle health, maintenance costs, and fuel consumption patterns.

Conclusion

The CarCare+ Application combines advanced AI technologies and real-time vehicle monitoring to simplify vehicle upkeep for users. By providing personalized maintenance reminders, predictive diagnostics, and detailed performance reports, the application helps extend the life of vehicles while reducing maintenance costs. This project aims to enhance vehicle efficiency, improve user convenience, and promote proactive vehicle management, ensuring a smoother and more cost-effective ownership experience.
