Overview

The CarCare+ mobile application is designed to help car owners manage the maintenance and servicing of their vehicles. It offers reminder features for basic tasks such as oil service times, tyre pressure monitoring, mileage tracking, inspection and tax payment due dates. The app also features a list of vehicle-focused products and additional features, such as scheduling service appointments, to help users keep their vehicles in top condition with minimal effort.

Objectives

The main purpose of the CarCare+ application is to provide users with a convenient experience by meeting their vehicle's health and maintenance needs. In the application, the user aims to simplify the process by indicating important things such as vehicle mileage tracking, brake pad wear status, air filter status, maintenance reminders to the user. In addition, the application sends notifications to the user about vehicle inspection time, tax payments, traffic ticket inquiries. In addition, it is aimed to provide convenience to the user by displaying the vehicle-related products in the store section. As a result, our main goal is to increase the safety of the vehicle, ensure that the vehicle is used for a long time, and provide the user with a trouble-free management of the vehicle.

Features

  • Brake Pads and Tyre Pressure Monitoring: It allows access to information about the tyres and brake pads of the vehicle through the application.
  • Exhaust Emission and Vehicle Inspection Time: Checks the exhaust emission level and sends notifications about the required inspection dates for the vehicle.
  • Engine Oil Status and Air Filter Tracking: It sends notifications to users at regular intervals when the air filter and engine oil change time is approaching.
  • MTV and Payment sections and Traffic fine query section: It guides users to query regular payments such as Motor Vehicle Tax insurance, maintenance and traffic fines.
  • Store and Reporting Section: It lists the products that car owners can buy only for cars and provides reports on issues such as the general condition of the vehicle, maintenance costs and fuel consumption.
  • Notifications and Reminders: A system that sends regular reminders to the user such as maintenance time, payment time.

Conclusion

CarCare+ aims to revolutionize the way people service or care for their vehicle. The application marries real-time data processing with sensor connectivity and intelligent notification systems to offer an all-in-one suite for mileage tracking, tire pressure tracking, and other maintenance activities that are considered vital for any vehicle. Complete with API integrations for both payments and inspections, along with personalized reminders and an intuitive interface, CarCare+ makes the complexities of vehicle management that much simpler.

CarCare+ helps users to maintain their vehicles more efficiently while contributing to longer life and safety. Convenience guaranteed and efficiency assured. With its development, this application will have enormous potential to become an inseparable tool for any vehicle owner, enriching everyday driving and making the maintenance task less stressful.
